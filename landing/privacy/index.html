Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Recall ("the extension") is designed to help users search and rediscover previously viewed web content using AI-powered summaries. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed and how it is used.

---

1. Information We Process

Recall processes content from web pages that users actively view and engage with. This may include:

  • Page content (text from articles or webpages)
  • Page titles and URLs
  • Derived summaries and embeddings generated from page content

The extension does not process pages indiscriminately. Content is only captured after meaningful user interaction (such as time spent on a page), and certain sensitive domains (such as email, banking, and document services) are excluded.

---

2. How Information Is Used

The processed data is used solely to:

  • Generate summaries of visited pages
  • Enable semantic search across previously viewed content
  • Improve the relevance and usefulness of search results
---

3. Data Storage

Summaries, embeddings, and related data are primarily stored locally within the user's browser using local storage technologies (such as IndexedDB).

Some data may be temporarily transmitted to external services for processing (e.g., generating summaries or embeddings), but is not retained beyond what is necessary to provide functionality.

---

4. Data Sharing

Recall does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data may be processed by third-party services strictly for functionality (such as AI processing), and only to the extent required to provide the extension’s features.

---

5. User Control

Users can:

  • Remove stored data by clearing extension storage
  • Uninstall the extension at any time
---

6. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to ensure that data is handled securely and only used for its intended purpose.

---

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
